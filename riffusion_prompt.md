# Prompt que gera um prompt para utilizar na plataforma [Riffusion](https://www.riffusion.com/)

OBS: O prompt utilizado em `<exemplo></exemplo>` é do [Márcio Campos](https://suno.com/@marciocampos)

```
<tema>
{COLOQUE AQUI UM TEMA}
</tema>

<exemplo>
<SONG_PROMPT>
   
   <SONG_DETAILS>
      [STYLE: Melodic Techno]
      [MOOD: Deep, Hypnotic, Introspective]
      [INSTRUMENTATION: Deep rolling bassline, atmospheric pads, minimal percussion, evolving synths, ethereal vocal layers]
      [TEMPO: 124 BPM]
      [PRODUCTION: Clean mix, analog warmth, layered transitions, smooth dynamic range]
      [INFLUENCES: Jan Blomqvist, Stephan Bodzin, Tale of Us]
   </SONG_DETAILS>
   
   <INSPIRATION>
      [TEXTURE: Smooth and airy, analog synth warmth, deep evolving drones]
      [VIBE: Floating, cinematic, immersive]
      [SONIC ELEMENTS: Organic percussion, pulsating bass, granular delays]
   </INSPIRATION>
   
   <SONG_MODULES>
      <INTRO>
         [Slow atmospheric pad introduction, evolving filter sweeps, deep bass pulse entering gradually]
         [INTERVAL: 4, 6, 8, 5]
      </INTRO>
      
      <BUILD_UP>
         [Percussion elements become more present, hi-hats and claps adding momentum]
         [Subtle vocal echoes emerging, synth arpeggios starting to take form]
         [INTERVAL: 5, 7, 4, 6]
      </BUILD_UP>
      
      <DROP>
         [Main bassline locks in, hypnotic synth melody layered on top, reverb-drenched vocals enhancing depth]
         [Rhythmic energy intensifies, percussion patterns dynamically shifting]
         [INTERVAL: 6, 5, 3, 7]
      </DROP>
      
      <BREAKDOWN>
         [Energy fades, lush pads take over, single note bass holding space]
         [Vocals become more ethereal, drenched in reverb and delay, transitioning into final drop]
         [INTERVAL: 4, 3, 5, 7]
      </BREAKDOWN>
      
      <DROP_2>
         [Return of full arrangement with added complexity, uplifting progression towards climax]
         [Layered melodies interplaying, evolving rhythmic modulations]
         [INTERVAL: 7, 4, 6, 5]
      </DROP_2>
      
      <OUTRO>
         [Gradual fade of bassline, atmospheric reverbs carrying the final harmonics]
         [Ethereal echoes creating a drifting sense of closure]
         [INTERVAL: 3, 5, 7, 4]
      </OUTRO>
   </SONG_MODULES>
   
   <TECHNICAL_TREATMENT>
      [MIX: Spacious, dynamic, warm analog texture]
      [REVERB: Deep, long tail on vocals and pads, subtle reflections on percussion]
      [COMPRESSION: Light glue compression, smooth transient shaping]
      [DISTORTION: Gentle saturation on bass and synth layers]
      [VOCAL PROCESSING: Ethereal, stereo spread, lush reverbs with sidechain compression]
   </TECHNICAL_TREATMENT>
   
   <OUTPUT>
      [VERSION: 1.0]
      [VARIANTS: Generate 3 variations with slight differences in bassline groove and synth arrangement]
      [REFINEMENT: Adjust vocal presence, tweak percussion balance, enhance spatial depth]
   </OUTPUT>
   
</SONG_PROMPT>
</exemplo>

<tarefa>
1. Refaça o prompt do exemplo de acordo com o tema. Lembrando que as instruções do prompt iniciarão com colchetes e terminarão com colchetes, delimitando todas as informações necessárias do prompt, tendo como formato de saída em markdown: [INTRO], [VERSE 1], [VERSE 2], [CHORUS], [CHORUS], [VERSE 1], [VERSE 2], [BRIDGE], [CHORUS], [CHORUS] e {End}. No máximo 2000 caracteres.

2. Traduza o prompt final para o inglês com o mesmo formato de saída em markdown.
</tarefa>
```
